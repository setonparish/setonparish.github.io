Seton Parish Festival

Thank you!

Our previous Seton Parish Festival was held September 15 - 17, 2017.

We had beautiful weather the entire weekend and enjoyed spending time with our parishioners and the entire surrounding community.

We celebrated with Parrots of the Caribbean, cheered for Reelin' in The Years, and rocked out with The Reaganomics. We ate a lot of amazing food, screamed on some rides, and won some raffles.

The dates for the next festival have already been set!

Our 29th annual festival will be held September 7 - 9, 2018 and we cannot wait to celebrate again with you and your family.
